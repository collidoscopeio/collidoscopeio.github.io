About Collidoscope

Collidoscope is driven by our vision to make digital sound and music more palpable, immediate, and magical for both performers and audience.

Collidoscope is an interactive musical instrument developed by Ben Bengler and Fiore Martin in March 2015. Fill it with sound and explore, and in the next moment, play and perform it like a musical instrument.

Amongst others, Collidoscope has been exhibited at Sonar+D (Barcelona, Spain) 2015 and 2016, at the Digital Design Weekend 2015 at the Victoria and Albert Museum (London, UK), and at Create Your World 2016 at Ars Electronica (Linz, Austria).

Collidoscope is not for sale. In a project supported by the Centre for Digital Music at Queen Mary University of London, we developed a new prototype and released the source code and hardware specifications under a free and open license. More information about the open source version of Collidoscope can be found on the code and documentation repository.
